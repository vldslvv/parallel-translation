Cogito, ergo sum.
I think, therefore I am.

Veni, vidi, vici.
I came, I saw, I conquered.

Dum spiro, spero.
While I breathe, I hope.

Alea iacta est.
The die is cast.

Carpe diem.
Seize the day.

Omnia mutantur, nihil interit.
All things change, nothing is destroyed.

Fortes fortuna adiuvat.
Fortune favors the bold.

Errare humanum est, perseverare diabolicum.
To err is human, to persist (in error) is diabolical.

Si vis pacem, para bellum.
If you want peace, prepare for war.

In vino veritas.
In wine there is truth.

Dum differtur vita transcurrit.
While you are putting it off, life is passing by.

O tempora!
Oh what times!

O mores!
Oh, the manners!

Quid est veritas?
What is truth?

"Amat victoria curam," ut Publilius Syrus dixit.
"Victory loves preparation," as Publilius Syrus said.

Nusquam est qui ubique est.
Nowhere is he who is everywhere.

Omnia, Lucili, aliena sunt, tempus tantum nostrum est.
All things, Lucilius, are another's, but time is our own.

Unus pro omnibus, omnes pro uno.
One for all, all for one.

Lupus non timet canem latrantem.
The wolf does not fear the barking dog.
